her name, her story

Starr

From "Star" — a light that guides, and one that shines uniquely on its own.

Azrielle

From the Hebrew "Azriel" — meaning "God is my help."

Together, Starr Azrielle represents a radiant light whose life is guided by God's grace and whose presence inspires hope, love, and joy to those around her.

Two years ago, we welcomed her big sister, Sky. When our second daughter arrived, the name felt like it had already been written for her — Starr, to shine alongside the Sky that came before her. Together, they are the sky and the light within it.
